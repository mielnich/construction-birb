Construction Birb

About Construction Birb

Construction Birb is a DND-like character with awful stats. The avian creature doesn't use strength or agiltiy to fight in battle, but instead focuses on scouting as well as singing to participate with his fellow adventurers. The bird does not know how to do any construction; he just wears a cute hat.
